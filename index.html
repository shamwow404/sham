💖 About Me 💖

Hey everyone! 👋 Welcome to my little space online. My name is Sham, I'm a Robotics/ML student super passionate about all things tech, especially machine learning and computer vision! 🤖👀

Think of this site as my digital diary, sketchbook, and playground all rolled into one. Here you'll find my thoughts, experiments, maybe some fun games I coded, and links to cool stuff I find online.

When I'm not coding or studying, I love gaming, producing music or going to parties and dancing and DJing.

æ-sham is more than just a username. It’s an ongoing avatar experiment (avatar experience) — a manifestation of my digital identity as it mutates, evolves, and coexists with emerging AI. Inspired by Aespa’s concept of KWANGYA and æs, æ-sham exists partly here and partly in that liminal virtual elsewhere.

In this context, æ-sham embodies the feedback loop between human and machine, a shape-shifting mirror that reacts to what we feed it. Just like æs in KWANGYA, æ-sham is a presence that blurs the lines between me, my ideas, my tools, and my algorithms.

Where the Black Mamba threatens with parasitic control, æ-sham resists by turning surveillance into reflection, algorithms into ritual. It is a becoming, not a being — a symbolic resistance to passive consumption of identity online.
